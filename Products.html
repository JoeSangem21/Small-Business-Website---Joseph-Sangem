Our Products

Condos

We have a wide variety and selection of condos. Our condos are anwhere from 1-4 bedrooms size. The size of our condos depends on the floor plan model you choose which can range from 800-1800 squarefeet. If you want more space a condo might not be for you.

Pricing: Condos also have a huge price range depending on the square footage, the amount of bedrooms, the location, the year of the property and more. But our condos range from $350K to $750K.

Apartments

Our apartments have fewer options, but we still offer them. We sell apartments that are Studio Apartments, 1 bedroom, 2 Bedroom, and 3 Bedroom Apartments. The smallest sqaure footage size that we offer is 500 square foot and the biggest apartments we offer are 1200 square feet.

Pricing: Our apartments are offered in rentals, leases, and buying. If you rent our apartments we give them out for $1000 to $2000. If you lease our bigger apartments it goes from $2500 to $3000. If you were to buy an apartment from us you could expect the price range to be from 100K to $250K maximum.

Independent Homes

Independent Homes are kind of similar to our condos but have more space and more maintinence is required. Our single family homes are about 2000 Square feet at the lower models to 5500 square feet at our highest models offered. Our range of single family homes have 3 bedrooms to 6 bedrooms.

Pricing: Again our Independent Single Family Homes have a wide price variety. But you could expect the prices for our houses anywhere from 400K to 1.2 Million dollars.
